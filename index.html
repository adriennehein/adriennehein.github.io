Adrienne Hein
Full-Stack Web Developer

I'm a full-stack web developer with a passion for design and good UX/UI. Since childhood I've always been drawn to the artistic side of things, which eventually led me to coding in adulthood. For me, coding is about being open-minded and creative about problem solving.

After six years working for a high-end retailer, I have acquired extensive client-facing experience, and learned the importance of staying level-headed in stressful situations.

I graduated college in 2011 with a B.A. Summa Cum Laude, and attended LEARN Academy in April 2018. When I find time, I love watching a good movie, volunteering, and exploring new parts of the city.

I built this page from scratch as a learning exercise and to reinforce concepts I've already learned. I know I still have a lot to learn, but I'm pretty sure that's the exciting part!
